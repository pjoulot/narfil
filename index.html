About Me

I am a 2D Artist

Do you know that feeling when time flies because you're having fun? That's what I feel when I'm drawing. For me, drawing is the easiest (and the most effective) way to solve a problem or explain an idea.

Animation movies, Videogames and illustrations from books have always bewitched me, I could spend hours immersed in those images, absorving their stories and learning their (visual) language.

I discovered Photoshop by accident and fell in love with digital illustration, that is why I ended up studying illustration from a traditional approach at the School of arts in Toledo (Spain) then I decided to move to Madrid where I signed up for the Videogame Design and Developement degree at Camilo Jose Cela University where I developed my skills in digital art.

Nowadays I live in Toledo with my partner in crime Philippe Joulot who has created this website.
